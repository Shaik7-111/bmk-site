Our Legacy Since 1875

Founded in Nandalur in 1875, the Bepari family has built one of South India's most respected wholesale grocery businesses. For generations, we have stood by the values of honesty, fairness, and consistency.

Today, under our trademark brand BMK Double Stars, we continue to deliver quality products in bulk across South India, with a vision to expand across the nation.
