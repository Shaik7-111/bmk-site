Our Heritage & Journey Since 1875

The roots of our business trace back to 1875, when the Bepari family began its humble journey in Nandalur. Guided by principles of trust, integrity, and hard work, the family steadily built a reputation for fairness and reliability in trade. Over generations, what began as a small venture grew into a respected wholesale business, supplying communities and merchants across South India. Each generation has carried forward not only the business but also the deep values and traditions instilled by our forefathers. This unbroken lineage of dedication and service stands as the true foundation of our success.
